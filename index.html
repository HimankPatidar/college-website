Government Ram Chandra Khaitan Polytechnic Collage Jaipur

Govt. Ram Chandra Khaitan Polytechnic Collage was established in the year 1978 with the motto of imparting professional education to the student. Collage is offering three year diploma courses in the Seven branches i.e Architectur, Computer Sc. & Engg, Electronics Engg, Mechanical Engg, Mechanical Production & Mechanical RAC. Civil Engg has already started from July 2012
