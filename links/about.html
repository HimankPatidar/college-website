Vision&Mission

VISION OF THE INSTITUTE

To create excellent professional ecosystem in the institute to fulfill the challenging needs of the industry and society as a whole.

MISSION OF THE INSTITUTE

Enable the students to excel in their academic pursuits and make them sensitive to the needs of progressive industrial world.
To provide need based quality education to students and faculty by establishing link with industries, government establishments, non-government organizations, alumni etc.
To update the knowledge and skill of the students and faculty members to meet the fast evolving technological challenges.
To develop holistic personality of the students so that they can contribute effectively and efficiently in their respective professional career.
To orient every activity in the institute towards infusing ethical values, team spirit, self-confidence in the individuals in order to contribute to the socio- economic development of the country.

About Govt. R.C. Khaitan Jaipur

Government Ram Chandra Khaitan Polytechnic College (Co-Ed.) was established in the year 1978 with the motto of imparting Technical education to the boys and girls of Rajasthan and other states of the country. Our college is situated 4 kM from Railway station Gandhi Nagar Jaipur, 7 kM from Air port and 8.5 kM from Bus stand(Sindhi Camp)

The institute is governed by Directorate of Technical education (DTE), Jodhpur, Government of Rajasthan. The diploma courses are recognized by the All India Council of Technical Education ( AICTE), New Delhi. The examinations are conducted by the Board of Technical Education Rajasthan, Jodhpur


The total student strength of our college is around 1000 together with seven technical departments (Mechanical, Mechanical Production, Mechanical RAC, Computer Science, Electronics, Civil, and Architecture) , Humanities Department & a well equipped library.


The total land area available to the institute is 8.09 hectares from which the various departments buildings have been constructed comprising class rooms, laboratories, conference hall, canteen / common room/ exhibition room / display room and parking etc.


Our College also provides additional facilities like sports facilities, Generator Back-Up facility, internet facility, playground, seminar hall, separate canteen, conference hall, 50 kw solar plant, Wi-Fi and Internet facility in college premise


Faculties are associated with different professional bodies like ISTE, IEI, ISHARE, Indian Road Congress etc. ISTE faculty chapter and Alumni association are formed in the institute.


Faculties are highly qualified with Ph.D. (14) ; M.E/M.Tech (35); B.Tech and M.Sc degrees


Presently total number of working faculty is 84.
