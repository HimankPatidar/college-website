AANANDAM

(AANANDAM - JOY OF LIVING)

"Temporary happiness may be in acquiring things but Permanent-long lasting happiness (AANAND) comes from giving, sharing and caring for someone"

'AANANDAM - the joy of giving and sharing' is to be instilled in students through a credited course, from session 2020-21 onward. AANADAM COURSE will help turning our student, the future of nation into responsible citizen. This concept will motivate students to undertake a daily act of goodness and charity that will infuse the habit of community service in students. The students (individually and in groups) will undertake the act of goodness for our nature and society, with the help of faculties.
